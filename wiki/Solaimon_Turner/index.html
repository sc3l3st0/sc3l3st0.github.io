Solaimon Turner went to high school at the Park School of Baltimore[1]. While there, he was introduced by a mutual friend named Tanner to his future waifu, Ange Ushiromiya[1]. Sadly, the two separated citing irreconcilable differences[2] after Solaimon joined the fraternity Beta Kappa Phi at the College of Wooster. Nobody can really be sure what Solaimon's up to. He's probably doing something lame[2].
